---
name: Blood on the Clocktower Scoring
description: Point-based scoring system for BotC seasons with tiered prizes
type: feature
---
Points = (games_played × 1) + (wins_evil × 2) + (wins_good × 1)
- Storyteller counts as 1 game played but no bonus points
- Tiebreaker: win % (excluding storyteller games)
- Prize tiers: 1-3rd R$75, 4-6th R$50, 7-10th R$30
- Scripts: Trouble Brewing (beginner), Bad Moon Rising (intermediate)
